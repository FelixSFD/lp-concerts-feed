App for iOS

You can install the new app, so you can have widgets on your homescreen to show the next concert and get push notifications about upcoming concerts.

Features

  • Widgets with a countdown to a Linkin Park show. Either...
    • ... next concert
    • ... next concert you have bookmarked
    • ... next concert you are planning to attend
  • Get push-notifications as reminders or when the schedule is confirmed
  • Get the weather forecast for your next concert
  • Share the countdown to your next concert with your friends
  • List of all upcoming concerts
  • bookmark concerts
  • Map of all upcoming concerts (and past concerts since 2024)
