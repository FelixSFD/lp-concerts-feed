== Show Notes ==

* Linkin Park's ''[[From Zero]]'' countdown timer of 10 minutes took place before the band went on stage. The pre-show song over the PA system was 'The Metro' by Berlin.
* \"[[Cut The Bridge]]\" and \"[[Good Things Go]]\" were rotated back into the set for the first time in six shows, the last full performance of both being in [[Live:20250508|2025.05.08 Greenville, South Carolina]]. Mike rapped Verse 1 of the song over the \"[[Bleed It Out]]\" bridge at the show prior in [[Live:20250616|2025.06.16 Hannover, Germany]].
* Mike and Emily spoke in German to the crowd before \"[[The Emptiness Machine]]\". Mike said, \"''I want to say thank you for your support! You really are the best and I had the best Currywurst ever, thanks for that.''\"
* Mike talked about arranging \"[[Where'd You Go]]\" with Emily for inclusion on tour before performing the song.
* Over ten moshpits erupted in the crowd during \"[[Two Faced]]\".
* Mike rapped the demo verse of [[Link:\"Lift Off\"|\"Lift Off\"]] during the extended portion of his solo medley. This was the first repeat of a verse for this part of the show, the last appearance of it being at [[Live:20250506|2025.05.06 Raleigh, North Carolina]].
* \"[[Given Up]]\" was rotated into the set, being performed for the first time in ten shows, the last performance being at [[Live:20250428|2025.04.28 Tulsa, Oklahoma]].
* Mike played the guitar riff for 'One' by Metallica on his synth over the intro to \"[[Overflow]]\" for a second show in a row.
* Mike had a fan pick a number between 1 and 3 to decide what style Colin would play the \"[[Numb]]\" intro in. Colin ended up playing the \"[[Numb/Encore]]\" intro at double speed.
* Mike rapped verse one of [[Link:\"A Place For My Head\"|\"A Place For My Head\"]] over the extended bridge of [[Link:\"Bleed It Out\"|\"Bleed It Out\"]].

== Other Notes ==

* This show was announced on November 14, 2024 with the ''[[From Zero World Tour]]'', exactly 27 years to the day of the band's first Xero show in 1997.
* For the second show in a row, the band performed a soundcheck on stage in the early afternoon. \"[[Over Each Other]]\" was soundchecked three times, with Mike on vocals on the second performance. \"[[Let You Fade]]\" was soundchecked twice.
* This was one of the biggest headlining shows Linkin Park has ever performed, with somewhere between 65,000 and 75,000 in attendance.
